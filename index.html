Sort your favorite Caravan Stories heroes! Pick your sources, and hit the Start button.

Certain options have details that you can hover to read.

Click the hero you like better from the two, or tie them if you like them equally or don't know they are.

There are around 250 characters, so it may take awhile to sort through all of them. Why not AFK grind in the background?



Keyboard controls during sorting: H/LeftArrow (pick left) J/DownArrow (undo) K/UpArrow (tie) L/RightArrow (pick right) S (save progress).

Before sorting: S/Enter (start sorting) L (load progress).

1/2/3 always correspond to the first/second/third buttons.
